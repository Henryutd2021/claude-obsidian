## Abstract gist

Evidence: The abstract says e-fuels can replace fossil fuels without some demand-side transformations, but their usefulness is constrained by climate effectiveness, costs and uncertain availability. It reports current mitigation costs of EUR800-1,200 per tCO2 and possible 2050 costs of EUR20-270 per tCO2, then recommends a merit order for sectors inaccessible to direct electrification.
