# Data Availability

## Verbatim statement

> The life-cycle analysis for passenger cars and trucks can be reproduced with the open-source tools carculator and carculator_truck. The electrolysis cost and efficiency data are available in ref. 59. All other data are available from the corresponding author on request.

Source: Nature article page for https://www.nature.com/articles/s41558-021-01032-7

## Lesson for my own DA statements

Lesson: Put every TEA input table that drives headline cost ranges into a citable repository. Avoid "all other data on request" unless there is a legal or licensing reason that is stated in the same paragraph.
