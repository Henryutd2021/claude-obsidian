# Data Availability

## Verbatim excerpt

> All data are publicly available at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.6989075.

Copyright-limited note: the full statement also identifies the data sources and currency normalization choices. It says the dataset covers historical solar capacity and component prices globally and for the United States, China and Germany, with inputs from IRENA, SEIA, LBNL, NREL, ERI, China Photovoltaic Industry Association and Fraunhofer ISE.

## Lesson for my own DA statements

Lesson: Put the archive DOI in the first sentence, then list each input source by region and variable. This makes reuse easier than a generic "available online" statement.
