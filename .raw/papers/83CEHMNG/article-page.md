# Article Page Snapshot

Source: Zotero child `CFSJIRLQ`, ScienceDirect Snapshot.

Title: Techno-economic assessment of renewable hydrogen production for mobility: A case study

Journal: Energy Conversion and Management, Volume 311, 1 July 2024, Article 118513.

Authors: Alberto Pettinau, Davide Marotto, Federica Dessi, Francesca Ferrara.

DOI: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.enconman.2024.118513

Highlights captured from the snapshot:

- Production and use of renewable H2 for urban buses in an integrated case study.
- 5 MW PEM electrolyzer fed by a 12.5 MWp PV plant to produce about 300 t/yr of H2.
- Capital cost of 20.3 M EUR and operating cost of about 520,000 EUR/yr.
- LCoH of 4.09 EUR/kg and plant profitability above about 5.25 EUR/kg green H2 selling price.
- Green hydrogen buses remain costlier than diesel buses in the studied urban transport case.
