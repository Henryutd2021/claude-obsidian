The Yang paper's +87% break-even sensitivity covers magnitude but not shape uncertainty.`

Add corresponding callout to `2023-NC-endogenous-learning-green-h2-europe.md` noting that Yang 2022 is a paradigm case of exogenous cost treatment and that Zeyen's result implies restatement of Yang's timing claims.
